The Open Group Architecture Framework


TOGAF is a tool to assist in acceptance, development, use and maintenance of enterprise architectures. It is an iterative process model based on existing architecture assets and best practices.

History

  • Maintained by The Open Group Forum
  • First developed in 1995 based on the architecture framework developed for defence information management known as TAFIM.
  • The framework can be used independently or alongside other frameworks that are specific for the concerned domain or enterprise.

Key Definitions

What is an Architecture?

An architecture is defined as the fundamental organisation of a system, embodied in its components, their relationship with each other and the environment, and the principles and guidelines that govern their design and evolution.
